ACME INDUSTRIES, INC. — Form 10-K, Item 1A (Risk Factors)
Fiscal year ended December 31, 2025  (synthetic example)

We operate in a competitive industry. Our customers may shift purchasing toward lower-cost alternatives, which could pressure our gross margins. We have a diverse customer base, with no single customer accounting for more than 8% of net revenue in fiscal 2025.

Our long-term debt totaled $420 million at year-end 2025, against $1.8 billion of stockholders' equity. Our debt covenants require us to maintain a consolidated leverage ratio of not more than 3.0x trailing-twelve-month EBITDA and a fixed-charge coverage ratio of not less than 1.5x. As of December 31, 2025 we were in compliance with all financial covenants and had $180 million of unused capacity on our revolving credit facility.

We derive approximately 62% of revenue from our Industrial Automation segment, 28% from our Connected Devices segment, and 10% from our Services segment. Our Industrial Automation segment serves the discrete manufacturing market, which is cyclical.

We are subject to various federal, state, and foreign environmental regulations. Compliance costs in fiscal 2025 totaled approximately $14 million.

We are involved in routine litigation incidental to our business, none of which our management believes is reasonably likely to have a material adverse effect on our financial position or results of operations.

The opinion of our independent registered public accounting firm on our consolidated financial statements as of and for the fiscal year ended December 31, 2025 was unqualified.
